Admissions

Join Our Learning Community

At Muel & Nuel Schools, we welcome students who are eager to learn, grow, and excel. Our admissions process is designed to ensure the best fit for both students and our educational environment.

Apply Now

Admission Requirements

Academic Records

Previous school transcripts and academic performance records are required for all applicants.

Age Requirements

Students must meet the minimum age requirements for their desired grade level. Class 6: 10-12 years, SSC: 14-16 years.

Entrance Assessment

All applicants must complete a simple entrance assessment to determine appropriate grade placement.

Parent Interview

Parents/guardians participate in an interview to discuss the student's educational goals and needs.

Admission Process

1. Application Submission

Complete the online application form with all required information and documents.

2. Document Review

Our admissions team reviews submitted documents and academic records.

3. Entrance Assessment

Students complete a brief assessment to evaluate academic readiness and placement.

4. Parent Interview

A discussion with parents to understand the student's learning style and family expectations.

5. Admission Decision

Notification of admission decision within 5-7 business days of completing all requirements.

6. Enrollment

Complete enrollment formalities and secure your child's place at Muel & Nuel Schools.

School Fees & Financial Information

Tuition Fees

Annual tuition fees vary by grade level. Contact our admissions office for detailed fee structure.

Payment Plans

Flexible payment options available including quarterly and monthly installments.

Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships available for outstanding academic performance and special talents.

Grade Levels Available

Kindergarten (Kg1 & 2, Nursery1 & 2)

Nursery and Kindergarten offer a caring start where children play, learn, and grow into confident learners ready for primary school.

Junior Secondary School (Class 1-3)

Comprehensive secondary education preparing students for higher learning and future careers.

Primary School Leaving Certificate (PSLC)

Advanced curriculum focused on Common Entrance and Secondary school readiness.

Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE)

Rigorous academic program designed to prepare students for all round success.

Important Dates

Application Deadline

Applications accepted year-round, but early application recommended for preferred placement.

Academic Year

September to June, with breaks for holidays and examinations.

Open House

Schedule a campus tour to experience our facilities and meet our faculty.
